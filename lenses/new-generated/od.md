They offer strategies for building coalitions, managing resistance, and creating conditions for sustainable change.

Throughout the work, Cheung-Judge and Holbeche emphasize the importance of ethical practice in OD work, addressing the responsibilities that come with the power to influence organizational systems and individual careers. The book's significance lies in its practical orientation combined with theoretical rigor. Rather than presenting OD as a set of tools to be mechanically applied, the authors present it as a sophisticated practice requiring continuous learning, reflection, and adaptation.
